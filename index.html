What I do

Student


I'm a High school graduate in Math, Physics, and Computer Science. In my free time, I enjoy sharing my knowledge through tutoring high school students. I also love challenge myself through working on hands-on programming projects and collaborating with other programmers on small and big projects.

Programmer & Author

As an Aspiring Computer Scientist, I spend a lot of time doing research on Computer Algorithms and Computer programming Languages. I like writing computer algorithms to solve complex mathematical functions such as Polynomials, Matrices, Factorial, Fibonacci Series, to name a few. I use programming Languages such as C++, Java, and Python to turn those algorithms into real codes that can be compiled and run on a computer.

Math & Coding Tutor

I started tutoring students online back in March 2019, after school closures due to COVID-19. A lot of students' education was stunted due to this pandemic, so I've been volunteerily tutoring students in Rwanda and around the world. I worked with Future Focus Rwanda , one of the best STEM companies here in Rwanda, and VirtualED, a non-profit profit organization based in the U.S. I offer both one-on-one and group classes through Zoom and Google Meet. For more information or scheduling a class with me, contact me here.
